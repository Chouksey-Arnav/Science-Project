📋 How to Play

🎯 Mission Objective

Work as a team to identify 4 dangerous pathogens (bacteria, virus, fungi, parasite), use correct prevention strategies, and prescribe the right treatment to stop the outbreak and save your school!

👥 Players & Setup

  • • 2–6 players share one device — sit together!
  • • One player controls the device; everyone else discusses out loud
  • • Estimated playtime: 20–30 minutes

🎮 Game Flow (per level)

  1. Scenario – Read the outbreak story together (30s)
  2. Team Huddle – Discuss 4 questions out loud (60s)
  3. Quiz – Answer 3 multiple-choice questions (25s each)
  4. Prevention Challenge – Pick the 3 correct prevention tools (45s)
  5. Consensus – ALL players must agree before confirming!
  6. Feedback – See results + real science facts

⚡ Teamwork Tip!

Never press a button without discussing with your whole team! The "Consensus" button ensures everyone agrees — true teamwork wins the game!

⭐ Scoring

  • • Correct quiz answer: 100 pts + speed bonus up to +50 pts
  • • Correct prevention tool: 50 pts each
  • • Perfect level (all 6 correct): +200 bonus pts
  • • Boss round answers: 200 pts each
  • • Low outbreak bonus: up to +500 pts

🌡️ Outbreak Meter

Starts at 75%. Correct answers lower it. Wrong answers raise it. Keep it as low as possible for max bonus points!
